Kanda Matsuri (Tokyo)

Kanda Matsuri or the Kanda Festival, is one of the three great Shinto festivals of Tokyo. This festival held in mid May in odd numbered years. Sotokanda, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo will be the place where the Japanese people celebrate this event. Consists of several other events held over an entire week but the main event happens over the weekend closest to May 15. This festival is a religious type of festival. The highlights are a day-long procession through central Tokyo on Saturday, and parades of portable shrines (mikoshi) by the various neighborhoods on Sunday.
