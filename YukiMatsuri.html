Yuki Matsuri, Snow Festival (Sapporo)

The Sapporo Snow Festival (Sapporo Yuki-matsuri) is a celebration held yearly in Sapporo, Japan, more than seven days in February. Odori Park, Susukino, and Tsudome are the fundamental locales of the celebration. The 2017 Snow Festival dates are 6 to 12 February at the Odori and Suskino locales and 1 to 12 February at the Tsudome site.


The Sapporo Snow Festival was begun in 1950, when secondary school understudies constructed a couple of snow statues in Odori Park. It has since formed into an extensive, marketed occasion, including awesome snow and ice designs and pulling in more than two million guests from Japan and over the world.
